Signs You Need Tire Service

⚠️

Uneven Tread Wear

Tires wearing unevenly may indicate alignment issues, improper inflation, or need for rotation.

⚠️

Visible Damage

Cuts, cracks, bulges, or punctures in the tire require immediate professional inspection.

⚠️

Low Tread Depth

Tread worn to less than 2/32 of an inch means it's time for new tires (check with a penny test).

⚠️

Vehicle Vibration

Noticeable vibration while driving may indicate unbalanced wheels or damaged tires.

⚠️

Frequent Pressure Loss

Tires that lose air pressure regularly may have slow leaks that need repair.

⚠️

Age of Tires

Tires over 6 years old should be inspected regularly, even if they appear to have adequate tread.
