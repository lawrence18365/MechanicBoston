Signs You Need Engine Diagnostics

⚠️

Check Engine Light

Illuminated check engine light indicates the onboard computer has detected a problem.

⚠️

Decreased Fuel Economy

Sudden decrease in MPG often indicates engine performance issues requiring diagnosis.

⚠️

Unusual Noises

Knocking, hissing, or backfiring sounds from the engine area need professional evaluation.

⚠️

Loss of Power

Vehicle feels sluggish, struggles to accelerate, or has reduced power output.

⚠️

Rough Idling or Stalling

Engine runs unevenly when idle or stalls unexpectedly while driving.

⚠️

Failed Emissions Test

Failed inspection due to emissions issues requires diagnostic testing to identify the cause.
