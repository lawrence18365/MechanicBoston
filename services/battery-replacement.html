Signs You Need a New Battery

⚠️

Slow Engine Crank

Engine turns over slowly or takes longer than usual to start, especially in cold weather.

⚠️

Battery Warning Light

Illuminated battery warning light on your dashboard indicates charging system issues.

⚠️

Electrical Issues

Dim headlights, power windows operating slowly, or other electrical components not working properly.

⚠️

Swollen Battery Case

Visibly swollen or bloated battery case indicates internal battery damage.

⚠️

Battery Age

Most batteries last 3-5 years. If yours is older, it's likely time for a replacement.

⚠️

Corrosion on Terminals

Excessive corrosion on battery terminals can prevent proper charging and may indicate battery issues.
