Signs You Need an Oil Change

⚠️

Dashboard Oil Light

Illuminated oil light indicates low oil level or pressure requiring immediate attention.

⚠️

Dark, Dirty Oil

Clean oil should be amber and transparent. Dark, gritty oil needs changing.

⚠️

Engine Noise & Knocking

Increased engine noise or knocking sounds can indicate insufficient lubrication.

⚠️

Mileage Threshold Reached

Most vehicles need oil changes every 3,000-7,500 miles, depending on oil type and driving conditions.

⚠️

Exhaust Smoke

Visible smoke from the exhaust can indicate oil is burning in the combustion chamber.

⚠️

Oil Smell Inside Vehicle

The smell of oil inside your car may indicate a leak or oil burning on hot engine components.
