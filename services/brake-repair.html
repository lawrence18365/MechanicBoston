Warning Signs You Need Brake Service

⚠️

Squealing or Grinding Noises

High-pitched squealing indicates worn brake pads. Grinding means metal-on-metal contact requiring immediate attention.

⚠️

Vibration When Braking

Steering wheel or brake pedal vibration typically means warped rotors requiring resurfacing or replacement.

⚠️

Soft or Spongy Brake Pedal

A pedal that feels soft or sinks to the floor may indicate air in the brake lines or a potential fluid leak.

⚠️

Longer Stopping Distance

If your vehicle takes longer to stop than usual, your brake system needs immediate inspection.

⚠️

Dashboard Warning Light

An illuminated brake warning light indicates a system issue that requires professional diagnosis.

⚠️

Vehicle Pulling to One Side

If your car pulls left or right when braking, you may have a stuck caliper or uneven brake pad wear.
