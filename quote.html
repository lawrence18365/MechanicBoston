Frequently Asked Questions

Is your quote service really free?

Yes, our quote service is 100% free for customers. We're compensated by our partner shops when you choose to get service from them.

How quickly will I receive quotes?

During business hours (8am-6pm), you typically receive quotes within 30 minutes. For after-hours requests, you'll receive quotes the next business morning.

Are the quotes binding?

Quotes are estimates based on the information provided. Final pricing may vary if additional issues are discovered during inspection, but our shops commit to transparency about any changes.

How do you verify mechanics in your network?

All mechanics in our network are verified for proper licensing, insurance, and certification. We also monitor customer reviews and satisfaction ratings.

What areas of Boston do you serve?

We cover all Boston neighborhoods including Back Bay, South Boston, Dorchester, Roxbury, Cambridge, Somerville, and surrounding suburbs.

What if I need emergency service?

For urgent needs, mark your request as "Emergency" and we'll prioritize it. Many shops offer same-day service, and we can also connect you with emergency towing if needed.
