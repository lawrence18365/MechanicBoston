About MechanicBoston

Connecting Boston drivers with trusted mechanics since 2025. Our mission is to make auto repair more transparent, affordable, and stress-free.

Our Story

MechanicBoston was founded by a team of Boston residents who were frustrated by their own experiences with finding reliable auto repair at fair prices.

After one too many unexpected repair bills and questionable service experiences, we decided to create a solution that would benefit both drivers and quality repair shops.

We started by building relationships with Boston's most trusted mechanics and repair shops - those with proven track records of quality, honesty, and fair pricing. Then we developed a platform to connect Boston drivers directly with these pre-vetted service providers.

Today, MechanicBoston helps thousands of drivers find the right mechanic for their specific needs, while providing shop owners with a steady stream of informed customers who value quality service.

Our Values

Transparency

We believe in clear communication and no surprises when it comes to auto repair. All shops in our network commit to upfront pricing and explanations.

Quality

We only partner with shops that meet our standards for certification, experience, proper equipment, and consistent customer satisfaction.

Fairness

We believe in fair pricing that reflects the true value of quality service, without markup or unnecessary work.

Community

We're proud to support local Boston businesses and help strengthen the community of skilled trades professionals.

How We Work

1

Vetting Partners

We carefully screen all mechanics and repair shops before adding them to our network. We verify licenses, insurance, certifications, and review customer feedback.

2

Matching Needs

Our system matches your specific vehicle and repair needs with shops that specialize in those services, ensuring you get the most qualified help.

3

Quality Assurance

We continuously monitor customer feedback and maintain strict quality standards for all service providers in our network.

4

Satisfaction Guarantee

We stand behind all services booked through MechanicBoston with our satisfaction guarantee and help resolve any issues that may arise.
