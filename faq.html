Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MechanicBoston's services, network of mechanics, pricing, and more.

General Questions

What is MechanicBoston?

MechanicBoston is a service that connects Boston-area drivers with qualified, vetted auto repair shops. We help you get multiple quotes from trusted mechanics for any auto repair or maintenance service, saving you time and money.

Is there a fee to use MechanicBoston?

No, our quote service is completely free to use. We're paid by our partner shops, not customers. There are never any fees or obligations when you request quotes through our platform.

How does the quote process work?

Fill out our simple form with details about your vehicle and repair needs. We securely share this information with qualified mechanics in our network who match your specific requirements. You'll receive multiple quotes, typically within 1-24 hours depending on the service urgency.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all neighborhoods in Boston including Allston, Back Bay, Beacon Hill, Brighton, Charlestown, Dorchester, East Boston, Fenway, Hyde Park, Jamaica Plain, Mattapan, Mission Hill, North End, Roslindale, Roxbury, South Boston, South End, and West Roxbury. We also serve some surrounding areas including Cambridge, Somerville, Brookline, and Quincy.

How quickly will I receive quotes?

For standard service requests, most customers receive their first quotes within 1 hour during business hours, and all quotes within 24 hours. For emergency services, we prioritize your request and typically have quotes within 15-30 minutes.

Am I obligated to use a shop that sends me a quote?

Absolutely not. You're free to compare quotes, ask questions, and choose the shop that best meets your needs—or decline all quotes if you prefer. There's no obligation or pressure when using our service.

Service & Pricing Questions

How do you determine service pricing?

We don't set prices—each shop in our network determines their own rates based on their costs, expertise, and market conditions. By getting multiple quotes, you can compare pricing, warranty terms, and turnaround times to find the best value. Our partner shops typically offer competitive pricing, often 15-30% below dealership rates.

What services can I request quotes for?

We can help you get quotes for virtually any automotive service including:

  • Brake repairs (pads, rotors, calipers, fluid flush)
  • Transmission service (fluid change, rebuild, replacement)
  • Engine repairs (diagnostics, tune-ups, rebuilds)
  • Maintenance services (oil changes, fluid checks, inspections)
  • Electrical system repairs
  • Suspension and steering work
  • A/C and heating system repairs
  • Body work and cosmetic repairs
  • Tire services (replacement, rotation, balancing)
  • Emergency repairs and towing

If you need a service not listed here, simply select "Other" in our form and provide details.

How accurate are the quotes I receive?

The quotes you receive are based on the information you provide and the shop's experience with similar repairs. For standard maintenance and common repairs, quotes are typically very accurate. For complex issues that may require diagnosis, shops will provide their best estimate based on the symptoms you describe, with the understanding that the final price may change once they inspect your vehicle. Our partner shops commit to transparency and will always discuss any potential price changes before proceeding with work.

Do I pay MechanicBoston or the repair shop?

You always pay the repair shop directly. MechanicBoston never collects payment for repair services. This ensures a direct relationship between you and the shop for all service and warranty matters.

Do shops offer financing options?

Many shops in our network offer financing options for larger repairs, typically through third-party providers like Synchrony or Affirm. When you receive quotes, shops will indicate if they offer financing options. You can also specify in your request if financing is important to you.

Our Mechanics Network

How do you select mechanics for your network?

All mechanics in our network undergo a thorough vetting process that includes:

  • Verification of proper business licensing and insurance
  • Confirmation of technician certifications (ASE or equivalent)
  • Business history and reputation check
  • Review of customer feedback and satisfaction metrics
  • Facility inspection to ensure proper equipment and capabilities
  • Agreement to our service quality standards and pricing transparency requirements

We regularly review customer feedback and maintain strict quality standards. Shops that don't maintain our standards are removed from our network.

Are all your mechanics ASE certified?

Yes, all shops in our network employ ASE-certified technicians (Automotive Service Excellence). Many specialize in specific vehicle types or repair categories with advanced certifications. For specialty or exotic vehicles, we match you with shops that have specific expertise and manufacturer training for your make and model.

Can I request a specific mechanic or shop?

Yes, if you already have a preferred shop in our network, you can specify this in your request and we'll prioritize getting a quote from them. You can also mention if you've had a good experience with a specific technician at a shop in our network.

Do you work with dealerships?

While our network primarily consists of independent repair shops, we do partner with some dealership service departments that meet our quality and pricing standards. If factory-specific service is important for your vehicle, please mention this in your request and we'll include appropriate dealerships in your quote requests.

What if I'm not satisfied with a shop's service?

Customer satisfaction is our priority. If you're not satisfied with the service you received, please contact us immediately. We'll work with you and the shop to resolve any issues under our customer satisfaction guarantee. In rare cases where a resolution can't be reached, we may offer assistance finding another shop to address the problems.

Guarantees & Warranties

Do repairs come with a warranty?

Yes, all services booked through MechanicBoston's network come with a minimum warranty on parts and labor:

  • Minimum 90-day warranty on all services
  • 12-month/12,000-mile warranty on most major repairs
  • Some shops offer extended warranties of up to 24 months on certain services

Specific warranty terms will be included in your quotes, so you can compare coverage when making your decision.

What is covered by the repair warranty?

Standard repair warranties cover:

  • Parts that fail during the warranty period (excluding normal wear and tear)
  • Labor costs to diagnose and repair warranty issues
  • Consequential damage caused by a warrantied part failure

Warranty coverage typically excludes damage from accidents, misuse, or modifications made after the repair. Each shop will provide specific warranty details with their quote.

How do I make a warranty claim?

If you experience an issue with a repair that should be covered under warranty:

  1. Contact the repair shop directly to explain the issue
  2. Schedule a time to bring your vehicle in for inspection
  3. Provide your repair invoice/receipt and warranty information

If you encounter any difficulties with a warranty claim, contact MechanicBoston customer service at (617) 555-1234 or support@mechanicboston.com, and we'll help facilitate the process.

What is your customer satisfaction guarantee?

Our customer satisfaction guarantee ensures that:

  • The final price will not exceed the quote without your prior approval
  • All work will be performed to industry quality standards
  • Shops will clearly explain all repairs performed and answer your questions
  • Any issues with the repair will be addressed promptly and professionally

If a shop fails to meet these standards, we'll work with you and the shop to make it right.

If I move to another part of Boston, can I still use the warranty?

Yes, repair warranties are honored at the original shop regardless of where you move within the Boston area. If you move too far from the original shop, contact us and we may be able to arrange warranty service at another shop in our network closer to your new location, depending on the specific repair and warranty terms.

Emergency Services

What counts as an emergency service?

We consider these situations as emergencies:

  • Vehicle breakdown leaving you stranded
  • Safety issues that make the vehicle unsafe to drive
  • Situations where you need same-day service
  • Towing needs for disabled vehicles
  • Vehicle not starting or severe operational problems

When you select "Emergency" as your service urgency, we prioritize your request for immediate attention.

How quickly can I get emergency service?

For emergency service, we typically connect you with available providers within 15-30 minutes during business hours and within 30-60 minutes during evenings, weekends, and holidays. For immediate towing needs, we recommend calling our emergency line directly at (617) 555-1234 rather than using the online form.

Is emergency service more expensive?

Emergency service may carry premium rates, especially during non-business hours (evenings, weekends, and holidays). However, our network partners commit to fair and transparent pricing even for emergency services. When you receive emergency quotes, any additional after-hours or rush service fees will be clearly indicated.

Do you offer roadside assistance?

Yes, through our network, we can provide roadside assistance services including:

  • Towing to a repair facility
  • Jump starts for dead batteries
  • Tire changes for flats
  • Fuel delivery when you run out of gas
  • Lockout service if you're locked out of your vehicle
  • Winching if your vehicle is stuck

For roadside assistance, call our emergency line at (617) 555-1234 for the fastest response.

What should I do while waiting for emergency service?

While waiting for emergency service:

  1. Ensure you're in a safe location, away from traffic if possible
  2. Turn on hazard lights to increase visibility
  3. Stay in your vehicle with doors locked if you're in a safe location
  4. If you must exit the vehicle, do so on the side away from traffic
  5. Keep your phone charged and maintain communication with the service provider

For situations that pose immediate safety risks, contact emergency services (911) before requesting roadside assistance.
