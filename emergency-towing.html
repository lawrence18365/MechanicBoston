Safety Tips When Waiting for Service

Turn On Hazard Lights

Activate your hazard lights to make your vehicle visible to other drivers.

Move to Safety if Possible

If your vehicle is drivable, move to the shoulder or off the road completely.

Stay in Your Vehicle

Especially on highways, remain in your vehicle with seatbelt fastened while waiting.

Use Reflective Markers

If available, place reflective triangles or flares behind your vehicle.

Contact Police if Needed

If you're in a dangerous location, call police non-emergency for traffic assistance.

Stay in Communication

Keep your phone charged and update the service provider about any changes.
